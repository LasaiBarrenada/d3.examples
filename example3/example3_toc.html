Metadata exploration: Eurostat table of contents


Explore the table of contents Eurostat online database.

This tree shows the different nodes of the Eurostat online database which is organised as a tree of data collections. A node represent either a single dataset/indicator or a group of such nodes (e.g.., various datasets/indicators that are derived from a the same data collection and/or survey). Nodes are represented by a unique code. This information is retrieved from the table of contents available from Eurostat bulk download facility.  


This template reuses and adapts the d3.js-based material developed by R.Schmuecker in the visualisation example of a "drag-and-drop zoomable, panning, collapsible tree with auto-sizing".
